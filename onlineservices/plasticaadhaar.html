How to Order Plastic Aadhaar Card

1. Visit the UIDAI official website

2. Click My Aadhaar then click Order Aadhaar PVC

3. Click Login thorugh aadhaar authentication

4. After logged in click Order Aadhaar PVC Card . It will show your details, then click next.

5. Accept the Terms and Conditions.

6. After made online payment. Your Aadhaar card will be placed order and the card will be dispatced through postal of your aadhaar address.
